The Best Movies According to Sandeep D S

My top 3 movies of all-time

3 idiots

This is my favourite movie which is based on the engineering life. It always says how we should be in our college life.

KGF

Says how we should value our promise. It shows a path where we should steadly move in our life to achieve what we have learnt.

Kodiyil Oruvan

In this movie I have learnt that achieving our dreams is more important than proving others how much we are capable.
